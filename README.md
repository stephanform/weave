## About WEAVE 2026

WEAVE 2026 is the STARS EU Alliance International Conference on **Multiple Transformation**. It brings together academics, researchers, educators, students, and European stakeholders to explore how universities – collaborating across disciplines, institutions, and borders – can weave together multiple transformation pathways into a shared vision for a sustainable, inclusive, and dynamic Europe.

The conference is structured around the nine **Thematic Interest Groups (TIGs)** of the STARS EU consortium:

| # | TIG | Transformation Strand |
|---|-----|-----------------------|
| 1 | Energy Transition | Sustainable energy research, green curricula, community energy projects |
| 2 | Art & Creative Industries | Cultural change, arts-based research, creative pedagogies |
| 3 | Entrepreneurship & Innovation | Innovation ecosystems, entrepreneurial education, university-industry collaboration |
| 4 | Sustainable Industry | Circular production, industrial symbiosis, applied research partnerships |
| 5 | Circular Economy | Systemic design, resource intelligence, lifecycle education |
| 6 | Digital Transformation | Digital literacy, data ethics, technology governance in higher education |
| 7 | Healthy Ageing | Demographic change, assistive innovation, interdisciplinary health research |
| 8 | Living Spaces | Urban resilience, smart mobility, place-based learning |
| 9 | Inclusion & Social Justice | Equity in higher education, participatory research, social justice and access |

**Cross-cutting Tracks:** Weaving Disciplines · Weaving Institutions · Weaving Communities

## Compiling the Document

The document is compiled with **pdfLaTeX** (run twice to resolve references):

```bash
pdflatex WEAVE_CfP.tex
pdflatex WEAVE_CfP.tex
```

### Requirements

The following LaTeX packages are required:

- `fontenc`, `inputenc`, `babel` – language and encoding
- `lmodern`, `microtype` – typography
- `geometry` – page layout
- `graphicx`, `xcolor` – graphics and colour
- `tikz` with libraries: `decorations.pathmorphing`, `patterns`, `positioning`, `calc`, `shapes.geometric`, `shadows.blur`, `backgrounds`, `fadings`, `arrows.meta`, `decorations.markings`
- `titlesec`, `enumitem` – section and list formatting
- `longtable`, `booktabs`, `tabularx`, `array` – tables
- `fancyhdr` – headers and footers
- `hyperref` – hyperlinks

A standard **TeX Live** or **MiKTeX** installation will include all required packages.

## Conference Format

**Two-day international conference** — in-person with hybrid option — in English.

### Day 1 – Laying the Threads
- Keynote: *Multiple Transformation and the European University – A New Paradigm*
- Panel: *Weaving Knowledge – Interdisciplinary Collaboration in Research and Teaching*
- TIG Strand Sessions (parallel tracks)
- Poster Exhibition & Networking Reception

### Day 2 – Weaving the Fabric
- Keynote: *Collaboration as a Transformative Force – Lessons from European University Alliances*
- Cross-TIG Synthesis Workshops
- Special Session: The Future of the STARS EU Alliance
- Closing Plenary & WEAVE Declaration
- Conference Dinner & Cultural Programme

## Submission Formats

| Format | Description | Length |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Full Paper | Original research with methodology and findings | 4,000–6,000 words |
| Short Paper | Work-in-progress or conceptual contribution | 2,000–3,000 words |
| Practice Report | Case study from teaching, research, or community engagement | 1,500–2,500 words |
| Poster | Visual presentation of research or project | Abstract: 300–500 words |
| Workshop Proposal | Interactive session (90 min) | Proposal: 500–800 words |
| Artistic Contribution | Creative or performative work on transformation themes | Proposal: 300–500 words |

All submissions must be in **English** and follow the conference template.
